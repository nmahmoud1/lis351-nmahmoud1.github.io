Stretch It Out!

Don't skip the stretch! Stretching keeps your muscles flexible, strong, and healthy. It’s also key to preventing injury and easing post-workout soreness.

Essential Stretches

  • Hamstring Stretch
  • Quad Stretch
  • Shoulder Cross-Body Stretch
  • Child’s Pose
  • Cat-Cow

Stretching Tips

Warm Muscles First
Stretch after a warm-up or at the end of a workout for best results.
Hold Each Stretch
Aim for 20–30 seconds per stretch. Don’t bounce!
Breathe
Inhale and exhale deeply to help your body relax into the stretch.
