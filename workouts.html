Find the Right Plan for You

Everyone starts somewhere! Below you'll find simple, structured routines for different fitness levels. Stick with it and progress at your own pace.

Beginner Workout

  • Bodyweight Squats – 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Push-ups (knee or full) – 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Walking Lunges – 2 sets of 10 reps (each leg)
  • Plank – 3 rounds of 30 seconds

Intermediate Workout

  • Jump Squats – 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Push-ups – 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Mountain Climbers – 3 rounds, 30 seconds
  • Side Planks – 2 rounds of 30 seconds each side

Advanced Workout

  • Burpees – 4 sets of 12 reps
  • Pull-ups – 3 sets of max reps
  • Pistol Squats – 3 sets of 8 reps per leg
  • Plank to Push-up – 3 sets of 10 reps
